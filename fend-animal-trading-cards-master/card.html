Coco

Coco often gets mistaken for a lion..

  • Favourite Food: Chicken
  • Favourite Toy: Bone
  • Best Friend: Misty
  • Favourite Place : Mum's Lap

Coco is a Long Haired Chihuahua. He may only be a small breed but he has a massive personality and can be a charmer with strangers. Long Haired Chihuahuas are good for apartment living but Coco does like to go on dog walks, though not too far before he signals its time to go back home by lying down in the middle of the pavement.
